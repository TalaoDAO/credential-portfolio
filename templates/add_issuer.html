 Authorize a new Issuer

An issuer is an individual or a company you have authorized to issue certificates confirming your skills, experience or abilities.

Those certificates are stored within your Identity with all information about the origin and issuance conditions needed to prove their reliability.

The issuer has to have an Identity to prove his own legitimacy.
